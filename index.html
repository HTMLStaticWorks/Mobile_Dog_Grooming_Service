GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide water or electricity?

No! Our vans are 100% self-sufficient. We carry our own fresh heated water and run on quiet solar-powered generators.

Do I need to be home for the appointment?

For the first appointment, we require you to be home so we can meet you and your pet. Afterwards, we can arrange latchkey services if you'll be at work.

How long does a grooming session take?

Usually between 1 to 2 hours depending on the size of your dog, their coat condition, and the specific package chosen.
