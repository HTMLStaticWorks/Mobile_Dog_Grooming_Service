OUR STORY

Because Pets Are Family

We believe every dog deserves a stress-free spa day, without the anxiety of a busy salon.

Safety & Comfort First

Traditional grooming salons can be overwhelming for many dogs. The noise of multiple dryers, being kept in cages, and the car ride can all contribute to stress.

That's why we bring the salon to you. Our state-of-the-art mobile vans are climate-controlled and fully self-contained. Your pet receives one-on-one attention from start to finish, just steps from their own front door.

  • Certified Groomers

    All our stylists are CPPS certified with ongoing training.

  • Premium Organic Products

    We use only the safest, tear-free, eco-friendly shampoos.

  • Cageless Environment

    Total one-on-one time. Never put in a drying cage.

OUR CORE VALUES

What Drives Every Groom

At Paws & Bubbles, we believe grooming is more than just a haircut—it's a health check and a bonding experience. Our values are baked into every bubble.

Patient Empathy

We see the dog first, not the schedule. Every pet is treated with the gentle patience they deserve, especially those with anxiety.

Master Excellence

Ongoing education in grooming techniques and safety standards is mandatory for our entire team of certified stylists.

Eco-Commitment

We use 100% biodegradable, tear-free shampoos and ozone-filtrated water to protect both your pet and the planet.

THE VANTAGE

A Spa on Wheels, Tailored for Them.

Our custom-built Mercedes Sprinter vans are more than just transport — they are precision-engineered grooming labs. Fully climate-controlled with specialized lighting to ensure the safest, most comfortable environment possible.

Hot Water System: Consistent, gentle water temperature for sensitive skin.

Medical-Grade Sanitation: Vans are UV-sanitized between every single appointment.
